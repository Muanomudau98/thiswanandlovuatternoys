Our Team

Our attorneys bring decades of combined experience across diverse fields of law. Each member of our team is dedicated to providing strategic solutions while upholding the highest ethical standards.

  • Adv. Thabo Thiswana – Senior Partner, specializing in Corporate and Commercial Law
  • Adv. Naledi Ndlovu – Senior Partner, specializing in Civil Litigation and Criminal Defense
  • Legal Associates & Support Staff – A passionate team working collaboratively to deliver results for our clients.

Our Mission

We aim to provide expert legal solutions that drive real impact in the lives of our clients and communities. With a balance of corporate expertise and a strong commitment to social justice, we stand for integrity, innovation, and inclusivity in the legal profession.

Our History

Founded in 1992 in Johannesburg, Thiswana & Ndlovu Attorneys has grown into a respected firm with offices in Cape Town and Durban. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include Corporate Law, Intellectual Property, Environmental Law, and Pro Bono initiatives aimed at making justice accessible to all.
